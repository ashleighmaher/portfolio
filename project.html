The Town of Drogheda

The local's guide to Drogheda town

Take a look below at what Drogheda has to offer.

Museums

Millmount Museum is located on an ancient hill in the heart of Drogheda overlooking the town.

The whole fort is now the Millmount Cultural Quarter, and features craft shops, art galleries, a café and an artisan food centre

Activities

Drogheda is a town with a constant buzz of tourism and events.

Take a trip to the local Scotch Hall Shopping centre for some retail therapy or visit Brú na Bóinne for some history.

Beaches

Take a stroll along the nearby beaches and enjoy the relaxing views and some ice-cream. Local beaches include Laytown Strand, Clogherhead and Bettystown which are loved by locals and tourists alike.
