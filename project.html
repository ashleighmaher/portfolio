The town of Drogheda

Things to do

Below are some ideas for what tourists can do when they visit Drogheda.

Millmount Museum

Millmount Museum is located on an ancient hill in the heart of Drogheda overlooking the town. The Millmount Cultural Quarter offers a wide ranging experience including the museum, martello tower and an array of craft shops, artists' studios and a café where tourists and locals from all ages or interests can find something they enjoy.

It is an important landmark of Drogheda and has been an enjoyable experience for those who have visited already.

Other Activities

There are many activities to be taken part across Drogheda whether you are there for shopping or to learn about the history of the town.

Take a trip to the local Scotch Hall Shopping centre for some retail therapy or visit Brú na Bóinne for some history.

Nearby Beaches

Take a stroll along the nearby beaches and enjoy the relaxing views and some ice-cream. Laytown Strand, Bettystown and Clogherhead are easily accessible by car(10 minutes or so) and bus from Bus Eireann in town.

They are local favourites and are well worth the trip.
